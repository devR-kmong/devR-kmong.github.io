Why CLICKR

CLICKR is a Web3 lifestyle app that lets you monetize your time.

Collect NFT tickets to participate in Time to Earn.

Paving the way for
T2E (Time-to-Earn)

CLICKR is a lifestyle app allowing users to monetize their time. Time is equally distributed amongst everyone, regardless of their age, gender, race, and more. The introduction of Web3 has allowed everyone to earn a share of the market that they take part in. Projects such as Axie Infinity and Stepn were pioneers of the P2E and M2E markets respectively. Compared to P2E and M2E, T2E (Time-to-Earn) is a relatively foreign name. However, users will likely have heard of projects such as the Pi Network. CLICKR joins them with the goal of expanding the T2E ecosystem by creating a gamified metaverse. Users will be rewarded for their time through actions on the CLICKR App. Monetize every second of your day through CLICKR.

CLICKR App Features and Functions
The CLICKR App is the center of the T2E experience.

Tickets:
CLICKR is conducting on early sign-up campaign through ticket sales on OpenSea. This will allow users to join CLICKR before the launch and take part in the phase-0 of the beta test before anyone else. Tickets will also give users an early advantage by increasing the mine rate. Each ticket will add points to the mine rate and give users 1 activation code. The number of Genesis Tickets sold will be capped at 1,000,000, and additional tickets will not be released. Lastly, ticket holders will be able to mint a Genesis Watch starting from phase-1.

Watch:
A Watch is the CLICKR equivalent to a Sneaker in M2E. Users that have a watch will be able to start filling up their reward gauges. Depending on the number and type of watches, the number of tokens and reward intermissions will vary. Watch reveal date will be announced in phase-0.

Activation Code:
Genesis Ticket holders in phase-0 will be able to invite users through activation codes that they will receive. Each ticket will give them 1 activation code. Only ticket holders and their invitees will be able to access the app in phase-0. Starting from phase-1, all users will be able to access the app. When a friend joins through the activation code, the inviter and the invitee will each have their mine rate increased. The referral levels and mine rate figures will be revealed in phase-0.

Reward Gauge:
Each user will have a reward gauge which will start filling up once they start the mining process. Once the gauge completely fills up, users will be able to click the claim button and receive rewards. The reward rate will be based on the number of tickets and invitees that have claimed rewards within the past day.

Users will have two ways to increase the gauge so as to increase the time between each reward claims:
(1) Use the automatic gauge increaser feature to send all accrued rewards to increase the gauge length. This is similar to automatically compounding the interest earned in DeFi protocols.
(2) Buy tokens from the market or save up rewards to increase the gauge length.

Beta Test
