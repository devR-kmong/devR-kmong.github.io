About Us

Your Best Partner

KMS Autoparts stands for "Korean Motor Service Autoparts" and values demands and requests from customers. KMS Autoparts is a company established on the basis of 20 years experience in the field.

We are specialized in European and Korean truck spare parts. Our range currently includes genuine brands - VOLVO, SCANIA, MAN, MERCEDES, IVECO, DAEWOO, HYUNDAI - and numerous OEM brands.

We Never Stop

At KMS Autoparts, all our team put their maximum efforts to expand market and develop range. In this way, we will never stop and grow together with our partners and customers.

If you are looking for the reliable and long-term partners, we are here.
