Innovation
Blockchain Payment

IBP is a CryptoCurrency transfer, exchange, and payment platform
based on the Klaytn and provides End to End services through
domestic and overseas online and offline merchants.

Feature

Rechargeable payment platform

By purchasing IBP, users can expect convenience of use, merchants can expect reduced fees, expand the utilization of blockchain, store transaction records in a distributed manner, and acquire the usefulness of transaction hedge when using after purchase

CryptoCurrency Direct Payment Franchise

Blockchain payment support using existing card payment system and Reflecting real-time Cryptocurrency price

DeFi(KlaySwap)

Provides exchange between Cryotocurrencies and Operate DeFi Pool

Adjustment operator

Provision of usefulness for the conversion of blockchain into legal currency. The user pays by blockchain, and the merchant provides settlement services to receive legal currency

Data Buyer

Obtain payment big-data from Franchise, marketing, and other businesses

Transpay(HMT)

Reduce risk by hedging and selling virtual assets held by customers in real time. In addition, a solution for transferring legal currency to an IBP prepaid card and providing financial services such as deposit/transfer
