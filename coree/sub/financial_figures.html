2024 as a Strategic Pivot Year

2024 represents a transition phase, during which Coree restructured its business units, streamlined product focus, and laid the groundwork for accelerated growth. While revenue declined temporarily, strategic realignment will enable explosive growth from 2025 onward.

Following a strategic adjustment in 2024, Coree projects a dramatic turnaround in revenue growth — from $280M in 2024 to over $2.83B by 2029. This 10X scale-up is powered by:

  • Breakthrough innovation in new drug development
  • Global expansion of Coree’s consumer health and digital health portfolios
  • High-margin monetization of genomic and microbiome
  • Cross-border licensing and commercialization
