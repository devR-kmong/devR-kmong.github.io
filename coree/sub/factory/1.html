No.6 Production Workshop
(Established in March 2021)

With a total construction area of over 1,800 square meters, it features granulation, premixing, large mixing, ten-column packaging, pillow packaging, and automatic heat shrink packaging processes. It is equipped with well-known domestic machinery, such as the ten-column strip packaging system from Jiangsu Jinyi, the granulation system from Chongqing Yingge, the mixing system from Zhejiang Xiaolun Pharmaceutical, and the automatic heat shrink packaging system from Shanghai Quanchi. It supports the production of 240 tons of probiotics annually, with a daily output of 800 kg.
